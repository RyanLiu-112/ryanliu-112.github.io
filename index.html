Research Profile

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Biochemistry and Molecular Biology at the UF Health Cancer Center. My work focuses on RNA modification biology and stress-response pathways, including RNA m6A dynamics during endoplasmic reticulum stress, cell surface-associated RNA and glycoRNA biology, and the role of ALKBH1-mediated RNA regulation in autophagy, glucose metabolism, and liver lipotoxicity.
